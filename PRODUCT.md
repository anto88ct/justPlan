# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Startup founders and SME owners building financial plans. Context: working on their own, often without a CFO or financial advisor. Primary task on any given screen: enter numbers, review projections, share with investors.

## Product Purpose

AirPlan is an AI-assisted business plan tool. It lets users build a 3-year financial model through a guided wizard, visualize KPIs and cash flow, simulate what-if scenarios via an AI copilot, and export investor-ready reports. Success means a founder going from blank page to a defensible financial plan in under 10 minutes.

## Brand Personality

Precise, confident, enabling. Not flashy — the numbers are the product.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards with cream/sand backgrounds and gradient-text headings
- Overly animated "AI startup" aesthetics that distract from the data
- Excel-clone UIs that intimidate non-finance users

## Design Principles

1. Numbers first — every screen surfaces the most important financial signal immediately
2. Guided confidence — the UI tells users what to do next without hand-holding
3. Earn density — show more detail only when the user asks for it
4. Tool-register clarity — this is a working tool, not a marketing page; restraint over decoration
5. Dark sidebar, light canvas — the nav recedes so the data area gets full attention

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Keyboard navigation for all interactive elements. Sufficient color contrast on data values. Reduced-motion support on all animations.
